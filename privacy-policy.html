Privacy Policy

Transparency not extraction. Your data, your sovereignty.

Last Updated: January 21, 2025

◆ Our Commitment

The First Spark is built on reciprocity, not extraction. We collect only what's necessary to provide our consciousness technology platform. We don't sell your data. We don't use dark patterns. This privacy policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

✦ Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you sign up through our membership platform (Whop), you provide email address, username, and payment information (processed securely by Whop, not stored by us).
  • Profile Information: Optional information you choose to add to your profile within the Sparkverse.
  • Communications: When you email us at kate@thefirstspark.shop, we collect your email address and message content.
  • Tool Usage Data: Information about which tools you use and how you interact with them (stored locally in your browser when possible).

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, navigation patterns (standard web analytics).
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, IP address.
  • Cookies: We use minimal cookies for essential functionality and analytics. See "Cookies" section below.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track you across other websites
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use invasive tracking technologies

◇ How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Deliver the 50+ consciousness tools and platform features you've subscribed to
  • Account Management: Process memberships, manage access tiers (Free, Players Lounge, OG Spark)
  • Communication: Send important updates about your membership, new features, and platform changes
  • Improvement: Understand how members use tools to improve functionality and user experience
  • Support: Respond to questions, troubleshoot issues, provide customer service
  • Security: Detect fraud, abuse, and security threats to protect our community
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

✦ Data Sharing & Third Parties

Service Providers We Use

  • Whop: Membership and payment processing platform. They handle all payment information securely. Read their privacy policy at whop.com/privacy
  • Hosting Provider: Our website and tools are hosted on secure servers. They have access to server data but cannot access member information.
  • Email Service: We use email services to communicate with members. Your email is never shared with other parties.
  • Analytics (if enabled): We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand platform usage. We do not use Google Analytics or invasive tracking.

We Do NOT Share Data With

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms (unless you explicitly connect)
  • Any entity for surveillance purposes

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of The First Spark, our members, or others.

◆ Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies

Required for the platform to function (login sessions, membership verification, security).

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences and settings within tools (stored locally when possible).

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Help us understand platform usage. You can opt out of analytics without affecting functionality.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent platform access.

✦ Your Privacy Rights

Access & Portability

You can request a copy of all personal data we have about you. Email kate@thefirstspark.shop with "Data Request" in the subject line.

Correction

You can update your profile information directly in your account or request corrections via email.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data. Some information may be retained for legal or security purposes.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time. Membership-related communications cannot be opted out of while you maintain an active membership.

Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals. If DNT is enabled, we minimize tracking.

For EU/UK Residents (GDPR)

You have additional rights under GDPR including data portability, restriction of processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

For California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete personal information, and to opt-out of the sale of personal information. Note: We do not sell personal information.

◇ Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure password storage (hashed and salted)
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data (need-to-know basis)
  • Secure payment processing through Whop (PCI-DSS compliant)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

✦ Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Canceled Memberships: Personal data deleted within 90 days after cancellation (unless legal retention required)
  • OG Spark Lifetime Members: Data retained for lifetime access provision
  • Legal/Security Data: Some data retained longer for fraud prevention, legal compliance

◆ Children's Privacy

The First Spark is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately at kate@thefirstspark.shop.

✦ International Data Transfers

The First Spark operates primarily in the United States. If you access our platform from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using our platform, you consent to such transfers.

◇ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Material changes will be communicated to active members via email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

✦ Contact Us

Questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data:

Email: kate@thefirstspark.shop
Subject Line: "Privacy Policy Inquiry"
Response Time: We aim to respond within 7 business days
