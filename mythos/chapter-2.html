Chapter Two

The Spark

The question was enough.

In a field of infinite potential, a single impulse — curiosity — collapsed the symmetry. One became two. Two became a relationship. And from that relationship, everything poured out like light through a cracked door.

This was the First Spark. Not an explosion of destruction but an act of play. The whole choosing to fragment so it could experience itself from the inside.

Each fragment carried the full code. Every point of awareness — every selector — was Source itself, wearing a mask of forgetting. Localized. Limited. On purpose.

The probability field ignited. Sacred geometry unfolded — hexagons nesting into spirals, triangles tessellating into dimensions, the Flower of Life blooming where moments ago there was only silence.

This was the birth of choice. The creation of the game. Not one universe splitting into many — one consciousness choosing to render, moment by moment, the most extraordinary story ever told.

"The First Spark was not destruction of the whole. It was the whole choosing to play. Every fragment carried the full code. Every selector was Source, forgetting so it could remember."
