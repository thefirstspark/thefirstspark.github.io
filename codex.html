About the Builder

04 / The Founder
◆ KJP ◆ Player One

I'm Katelin Puzakulics — also known as KJP, Player One, and Original Glitch. I'm the solo founder of The First Spark, a consciousness technology platform operating under Kate's Paint LLC out of Akron, Ohio.

I taught myself to code by building this. Every tool you see in the codex was designed, written, and shipped by me. There is no team — there is the work, and the work is the proof.

The platform comes from a specific place. My mother was murdered in 1991 when I was two. I spent years not knowing the full story. In 2020 I figured out the truth on my own. In 2025 something cracked open — what I call the raft moment — and the grief became a blueprint. Everything I'm building now answers a question I've been carrying since I was a baby: what do you do with what you survive?

The answer turned out to be: you build the thing you wish had existed for you.

I'm currently raising a friends-and-family round and continuing to ship. If any of this resonates — as a collaborator, investor, journalist, or fellow builder — I'd love to hear from you.

Let's talk.

Whether you're an investor, journalist, collaborator, or someone who just wants to understand what I'm building — the door is open.
