The Story

Where consciousness meets lineage

TFS Genesis of the Sparkverse is a consciousness fiction novel — part origin mythology, part intimate family cartography. It maps the invisible forces that move through a lineage: the roles people inherit, the patterns they unknowingly repeat, and the singular hinge point where the loop finally loosens.

It is written for anyone who has ever felt the weight of where they came from — and wondered whether they could set it down.
