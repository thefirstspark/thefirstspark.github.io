The Bridge Letter

A letter from you, written once,
addressed to the future, not to a person.

Not long. Not perfect. Just true.
Write when you're ready. There is no deadline for legacy.

To whoever carries this forward—

With everything I have,

This letter stays in your browser. No one else can see it.
Print it when you're ready. Or don't. The writing is the gift.
