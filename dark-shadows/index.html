This is not wellness content. This is not a healing journey in seven easy steps. This is an honest passage through the five weathers of grief — written by someone who walked through each of them and came back with the coordinates.

Most of what you find online about trauma, grief, and the dark places either sanitizes them or romanticizes them. Neither is useful when you are actually in one. You don't need to be told it's beautiful. You don't need to be told to breathe. You need a voice on the other side of the wall saying I know this room. I lived here. You will not be here forever, and while you are, I'll stay.

Each chapter does not tell you about an emotion.
It makes you feel the shape of it,
and hands you a way out.

The five pieces together form an arc — grief, rage, hollow, return, integration. They were built to be read in order if you have the time, or visited individually when a specific weather rolls in. Each one is interactive in its own way. Each one is a small room built specifically for the emotion it holds.

No email wall. No upsell in the middle. Nothing gets stored. This is a gift, placed on the internet, for anyone who needs it — and will still be here the next time the storm comes.
