The Lounge Protocol

I. Arrival

Close the door. Set aside the last conversation, notification, demand.

Purpose: Transition from external to internal operating mode.

II. Decompression

Notice what you're carrying. Name it without fixing it. Let the weight register.

Purpose: Acknowledge the real load before attempting to move.

III. Signal Check

Separate noise from signal. What actually matters right now versus what feels urgent?

Purpose: Restore accurate priority perception.

IV. Move Review

Assess recent moves without judgment. What worked? What didn't? What was learned?

Purpose: Extract insight without self-punishment.

V. Exit Clean

Leave with one clear next action. No overwhelm. No backlog. One move.

Purpose: Return to the game with precision, not panic.

The Inner Conditions

These conditions must be true inside the lounge. Not aspirations. Requirements.

  • No urgency. Everything here moves at the pace of clarity.
  • No performance. Nothing here is for anyone else.
  • No persuasion. You are not selling yourself on anything.
  • No comparison. Other players are irrelevant in this room.
  • No multitasking. One thing receives your attention.
  • No self-punishment. Assessment without cruelty.
  • No shortcuts. The process is the point.

The Player's Mirror

Questions for honest self-assessment. Answer before the next move.

  1. Am I reacting or choosing?
  2. What am I avoiding by staying busy?
  3. If I do nothing for the next 48 hours, what actually breaks?
  4. Who am I trying to prove something to right now?
  5. What would the version of me who already won do next?
